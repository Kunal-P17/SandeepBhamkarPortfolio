Turning Reel Into Real

From childhood doodles to cinematic masterpieces, my journey blends passion and profession. With a

Fine Arts degree from Pune University, I started with theatre and event set design, evolving through

collaborations with top art directors from film industry, Mumbai.


As the founder of Creators Media and Entertainment Pvt. Ltd., I’ve crafted visual narratives for reality shows,

film promos, web series, and ads, also working with industry giant like HBO, Warner Bros and Netflix. We

embrace diverse projects, turning reel into real, telling stories, and creating resonant spaces.


Join me where art meets ambition and imagination fuels innovation. Let’s connect and inspire!
